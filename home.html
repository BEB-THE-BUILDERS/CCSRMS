About CCSRMS
College of Computer Studies Room Management System

CCSRMS (College of Computer Studies Room Management System) is a web-based scheduling platform designed to streamline the management and assignment of classrooms within the College of Computer Studies at Gordon College. It provides department heads with a centralized interface to efficiently create, view, edit, and organize class schedules across multiple floors and rooms without conflicts. Built to enhance administrative accuracy and transparency, CCSRMS ensures that every room, time slot, and faculty member is properly coordinated for each academic term.
