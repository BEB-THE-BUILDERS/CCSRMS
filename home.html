1. Introduction

Welcome to the CCS Room Management System (CCSRMS), a web-based platform developed for the College of Computer Studies (CCS). By accessing and using this system, you agree to be bound by the terms and conditions set forth in this document. These terms apply to all authorized users of CCSRMS.

2. Purpose of the System

CCSRMS is designed exclusively for the management and scheduling of classrooms and laboratory rooms within the College of Computer Studies. The system enables authorized Department Heads to:

  • View and manage room availability across all floors
  • Schedule classes including lecture and laboratory sessions
  • Assign faculty members to specific rooms and time slots
  • Organize and monitor weekly room utilization
  • Filter and categorize schedules by program (BSCS, BSIT, BSEMC) and subject type (Major/Minor)

3. Authorized Users

Access to CCSRMS is strictly limited to authorized personnel of the College of Computer Studies. Only verified Department Heads with valid credentials issued by the system administrator are permitted to use this platform. Unauthorized access is strictly prohibited and may be subject to disciplinary action.

4. User Responsibilities

As an authorized user of CCSRMS, you agree to:

  • Ensure all schedule entries are accurate, complete, and up-to-date
  • Avoid scheduling conflicts by reviewing existing room assignments before adding new ones
  • Use the system solely for official room scheduling purposes
  • Not misrepresent course names, faculty assignments, or section information
  • Promptly update or remove outdated schedules to maintain system accuracy
  • Exercise care when using features such as drag-and-drop rescheduling or bulk deletion

5. Account Security

You are solely responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials. You agree to:

  • Keep your password secure and not share it with any other person
  • Log out of the system after each session, especially on shared devices
  • Immediately report any suspected unauthorized access to your account to the system administrator
  • Change your password periodically using the Change Password feature available in the sidebar

The CCS is not liable for any loss or damage arising from unauthorized use of your account due to your failure to keep your credentials secure.

6. Data and Privacy

CCSRMS stores the following data in its database:

  • Your account information (email address and encrypted password)
  • Room schedules including course names, section, faculty, day, and time
  • User activity metadata such as acceptance of terms

All data stored in CCSRMS is the property of the College of Computer Studies. Data is used solely for the purpose of room management and is not shared with external parties. You agree not to extract, copy, or reproduce system data for unauthorized purposes.

7. Prohibited Actions

The following actions are strictly prohibited and may result in account suspension or disciplinary measures:

  • Accessing or attempting to access another user's account
  • Deleting, modifying, or overwriting schedules belonging to other departments without authorization
  • Using the Danger Zone (Delete All Schedules) feature recklessly or without proper authorization
  • Attempting to exploit, hack, or reverse-engineer any part of the system
  • Inputting false or misleading schedule information into the system

8. System Availability and Changes

CCSRMS is provided on an "as available" basis. The system administrator reserves the right to:

  • Temporarily suspend the system for maintenance without prior notice
  • Modify, update, or discontinue any feature of the system at any time

Continued use of CCSRMS after any changes to these terms constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

About CCSRMS
College of Computer Studies Room Management System

CCSRMS (College of Computer Studies Room Management System) is a web-based scheduling platform designed to streamline the management and assignment of classrooms within the College of Computer Studies at Gordon College. It provides department heads with a centralized interface to efficiently create, view, edit, and organize class schedules across multiple floors and rooms without conflicts. Built to enhance administrative accuracy and transparency, CCSRMS ensures that every room, time slot, and faculty member is properly coordinated for each academic term.
